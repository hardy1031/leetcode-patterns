## Reflection

Yesterday I worked on binary search problems. "Binary search on answer space" was a new pattern I came across — it requires defining the range of possible answers first, then running binary search on that range. I also struggled a lot with rotated sorted arrays. Finding the minimum was straightforward, but searching for a target element was more challenging. The key is deciding which half to drop or keep. For searching in a rotated array, I had to focus on the sorted half — because updating the left and right pointers only makes sense on a sorted half.
