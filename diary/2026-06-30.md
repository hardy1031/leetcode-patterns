## Reflection

Started June 30 with a review session before tackling new problems. Re-solved 143 (Reorder List) and hit two bugs: first, not cutting the list after finding the middle with fast/slow, which caused the first and second halves to share a node. Second, using `slow.next` instead of `second.next` inside the reversal loop — since `slow.next` was already set to `None` after the cut, `next_node` was always `None` and only one node got reversed. The cut is critical and easy to forget.
