# 11. Container With Most Water

Given an integer array `height` of length n, find two lines that form a container with the x-axis that holds the most water. Return the maximum amount of water.

**Example 1:** height = [1,8,6,2,5,4,8,3,7] → 49
**Example 2:** height = [1,1] → 1
